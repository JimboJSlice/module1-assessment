Every great story has a beginning. For Kobe McDonald, that story is rooted in one of Mayo football's most iconic names. Before forging his own path, it's worth looking back at the brilliance of his father, Ciaran McDonald—a player whose vision, skill and passion captivated supporters throughout his legendary career in the green and red. This video celebrates the legacy that inspired a new generation, as Kobe begins writing the next chapter.

Kobe McDonald's first senior championship performance marked the arrival of one of Mayo's brightest young talents. Playing with composure, confidence, and maturity beyond his years, he gave supporters a glimpse of his immense potential. While his journey in the green and red will now continue in a different direction as he pursues a career in Australian Rules Football, this performance remains an important chapter in his Mayo story.
